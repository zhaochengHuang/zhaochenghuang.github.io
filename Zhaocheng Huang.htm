Zhaocheng Huang

Short Bio:

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at School of Electrical Engineering and Telecommunications, UNSW Sydney, supervised by Professor Julien Epps, working on the automatic speech-based assessment of mental state via mobile devices, closely collaborated with Boston-based startup Sonde Health.

I received my doctoral degree in 2018 for the study on speech-based emotion and emotion change in continuous automatic systems at University of New South Wales (UNSW Sydney), Australia , and received my bachelor degree from Harbin Engineering University, China, in 2013.
